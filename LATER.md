## Common parking-lot patterns

These are the things people commonly think they need at v1 but should defer:

- **Admin dashboard** — defer until you have ≥10 paying users
- **Multi-tenancy / orgs** — defer until you have a customer asking for it
- **Onboarding tour / tooltips** — defer until users complain about being lost
- **Email drip campaigns beyond welcome/receipt** — defer to post-launch
- **Granular permissions / roles beyond admin/user** — defer until needed
- **i18n** — defer until you have a non-English customer
- **Dark mode toggle** — pick one mode for v1
- **Custom email templates beyond essentials** — defer
- **Slack/Discord integrations** — defer
- **Public API for users** — defer until customers ask
- **Webhook system for users** — defer
- **CSV import/export** — defer until customers ask
- **In-app chat / support widget** — Intercom etc. defer to post-launch
- **A/B testing framework** — defer to post-Stage-13 PostHog work
